Important Safety Tips

🔥 Fire Safety

  • Never use elevators during a fire.
  • Cover your nose with a wet cloth to avoid smoke.
  • Check doors for heat before opening them.
  • Keep a fire extinguisher at home.

🚑 Medical Emergency

  • Check if the person is conscious and breathing.
  • Do not give food or water to an unconscious person.
  • Apply pressure to stop bleeding.
  • Call an ambulance immediately.

👩 Women Safety

  • Share your live location with trusted contacts.
  • Use well‑lit and crowded routes at night.
  • Keep emergency numbers saved on speed dial.
  • Trust your instincts — leave unsafe situations quickly.

🚗 Road Safety

  • Always wear a seatbelt.
  • Do not use your phone while driving.
  • Follow speed limits.
  • Keep a first‑aid kit in your vehicle.

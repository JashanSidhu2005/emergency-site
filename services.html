Emergency Services We Provide

Below is a list of verified emergency helpline numbers available across India. These services operate 24/7 and are essential for public safety.

  • Police (100) — For crime, theft, assault, or danger
  • Ambulance (102) — For medical emergencies
  • Fire Brigade (101) — For fire accidents
  • Women Helpline (1091) — For women’s safety
  • Child Helpline (1098) — For child protection
  • Cyber Crime (1930) — For online fraud or cyber threats
  • Disaster Management (108) — For natural disasters
  • Road Accident (1073) — For highway emergencies
  • Tourist Helpline (1363) — For travelers in India

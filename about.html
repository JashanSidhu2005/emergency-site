About This Project

This website was created to provide quick access to important emergency numbers in India. During emergencies, every second matters. This platform helps users quickly call police, ambulance, fire services, and other helplines.

Features include WhatsApp SOS, live location sharing and nearby hospitals & police stations. The goal is to make India safer with technology.
